HOW TO PLAY

LEARN ABOUT THE GAME

HOW TO PLAY IN PC:

- PRESS A, S AND D OR THE ARROWS TO MOVE THE PIECE.
- PRESS Q, AND E OR SPACEBAR OR ARROW UP TO ROTATE THE PIECE.
- YOU LOSE WHEN THE PIECES CATCH UP THE TOP BORDER.

HOW TO PLAY IN MOBILE:

- DRAG TO LEFT, RIGHT OR DOWN TO MOVE THE PIECE.
- DRAG TO UP OR TOUCH TO ROTATE THE PIECE.
- YOU LOSE WHEN THE PIECES CATCH UP THE TOP BORDER.

PLAY IT IN PC OR MOBILE AND ENJOY WITH YOUR FRIENDS OR SOLO!
